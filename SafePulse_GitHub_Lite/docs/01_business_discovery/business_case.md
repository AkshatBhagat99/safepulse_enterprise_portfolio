# Business Case

## Strategic rationale

School districts are under increasing pressure to respond consistently, document support requests, and provide leadership with clear operating metrics. The business need is not to replace professional judgment, but to improve the operating infrastructure around student support services.

SafePulse creates a single source of truth for operational case management. The platform supports executive oversight, school-level accountability, and day-to-day counselor productivity.

## Business objectives

| ID | Objective | Measurement |
|---|---|---|
| BO-01 | Standardize student support intake across all schools | 95% of requests captured through approved intake channels |
| BO-02 | Improve first response timeliness | Avg first response time reduced by 30% |
| BO-03 | Improve SLA compliance | SLA compliance increased to 90%+ |
| BO-04 | Reduce aged backlog | Cases older than 15 school days reduced by 25% |
| BO-05 | Improve executive reporting | Monthly district reporting cycle reduced from 5 days to 1 day |
| BO-06 | Improve counselor workload visibility | Utilization available by counselor, school, category, and term |

## ROI estimate

- Year 1 net benefit: -$230,000 after implementation cost
- Year 2 and onward annual net benefit: $850,000
- Payback period: approximately 15 months
- 3-year benefit: $2.55M
- 3-year net benefit: $1.47M
- 3-year ROI: approximately 136%

## Key risks

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Low adoption by school staff | High | Role-based training, office hours, workflow champions |
| Inconsistent historical data | Medium | Data profiling, standard category mapping, migration rules |
| Over-customization by school | Medium | District-level workflow governance |
| Privacy concerns | High | Strict access controls, audit logs, anonymized analytics |
| Reporting misinterpretation | Medium | KPI glossary and dashboard training |
