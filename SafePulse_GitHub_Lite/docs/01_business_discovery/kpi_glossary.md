# Business KPI Glossary

| KPI | Executive question answered | Formula source |
|---|---|---|
| Average Response Time | How quickly are schools acknowledging support requests? | First assigned/triaged timestamp minus request submitted timestamp |
| Average Resolution Time | How long does it take to close a case? | Closed timestamp minus submitted timestamp |
| Open Cases | What is the current operational workload? | Count of cases not in Closed or Resolved status |
| Case Backlog | What work is aging beyond normal operating expectations? | Open cases older than threshold by priority/category |
| SLA Compliance | Are schools meeting response and resolution commitments? | Cases within SLA divided by eligible cases |
| Escalation Rate | How often does work require escalation? | Escalated cases divided by total cases |
| Repeat Incident Rate | How many anonymized students have repeated cases in a term? | Students with more than one case divided by students with cases |
| Counselor Utilization | Which counselors are overloaded or underutilized? | Weighted active workload divided by capacity |
| Department Utilization | Which departments need staffing support? | Department workload divided by target capacity |
| School Performance Index | Which schools need operational attention? | Weighted index of SLA, backlog, response time, and reopen rate |
| Case Aging | Which cases are at risk of delay? | Days since submitted for open cases |
| Seasonality | Which months or terms show demand spikes? | Monthly case volume trend and category mix |
| Workload Forecast | What staffing demand is expected next period? | Historical volume trend with moving average forecast |
| Operational Efficiency Score | Is workflow improving over time? | Composite of SLA, response, resolution, backlog, and reopen rate |
| Case Reopen Rate | Are cases being closed prematurely? | Reopened cases divided by closed cases |
