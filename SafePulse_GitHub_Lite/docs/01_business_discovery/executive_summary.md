# Executive Summary

## Engagement context

SafePulse is a proposed enterprise platform for school districts that need stronger operational control over student support workflows. District leaders currently receive thousands of support requests each year across bullying reports, safety concerns, parent concerns, peer conflict, wellness checks, behavioral concerns, and related non-clinical support needs.

The current operating model is fragmented: requests arrive through multiple channels, assignment logic is manual, follow-up cadence varies by school, and leadership reporting is delayed. As a result, district leaders cannot reliably answer operational questions such as which schools need additional counselors, where response SLAs are failing, which departments are overloaded, and which workflow steps create bottlenecks.

SafePulse addresses this through an integrated case management and analytics platform designed around workflow intelligence, operational reporting, staffing visibility, and executive decision support.

## Business problem

The district does not have one standardized operating system for student support operations. Schools maintain disconnected spreadsheets, email threads, ad hoc forms, and local case notes. This creates four major problems:

1. **Slow response visibility:** Leaders cannot see which requests are waiting for triage or assignment.
2. **Inconsistent case handling:** Assignment and escalation rules differ by school and department.
3. **Counselor overload:** Staffing decisions are made without workload and utilization analytics.
4. **Weak executive reporting:** Board-level and superintendent reporting requires manual consolidation.

## Proposed solution

SafePulse combines:

- Case intake and triage workflow
- SLA-based assignment and escalation
- Counselor workspace and follow-up tracking
- School and district executive dashboards
- Operational KPI reporting
- Audit-ready status history and access controls
- Dimensional analytics model for Power BI/Tableau

## Expected value

| Value area | Current state | Future state |
|---|---|---|
| Response visibility | Manual checks and delayed updates | Near real-time intake, triage, and SLA monitoring |
| Counselor workload | Unknown until overload occurs | Workload balancing by school, role, and case complexity |
| Executive reporting | Manual monthly compilation | Automated dashboards by school, department, and term |
| Process quality | Inconsistent follow-up | Standardized case lifecycle and follow-up controls |
| Planning | Reactive staffing decisions | Data-informed staffing and capacity planning |

## Expected business outcomes

- 25–35% reduction in average first response time
- 20–30% improvement in SLA compliance
- 15–25% reduction in aged backlog
- 30–40% reduction in manual reporting effort
- Improved consistency in follow-up and escalation workflows

## Scope boundary

SafePulse does **not** predict or diagnose any medical, mental health, or personal condition. All data products are operational and administrative: workload, SLA, bottlenecks, staffing, case aging, process quality, and executive reporting.
