# Stakeholder Register

| Stakeholder | Role in program | Needs | Influence | Interest | Engagement approach |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Superintendent | Executive sponsor | District KPI visibility, board reporting | High | High | Monthly steering committee and executive dashboard review |
| Assistant Superintendent | Business owner | Operational performance by school | High | High | Biweekly decision forum |
| School Principal | School accountable owner | Case backlog, SLA breaches, counselor workload | High | High | School dashboard demos and UAT workshops |
| Operations Director | Process owner | Standard workflow, bottleneck visibility | High | High | Weekly project working sessions |
| Counseling Director | Functional owner | Counselor capacity, equitable workload | High | High | Requirements and workflow design workshops |
| Counselor | Primary end user | Simple workspace, case history, follow-up reminders | Medium | High | Persona-driven usability testing |
| Support Coordinator | Day-to-day triage user | Intake queue, assignment controls, escalations | Medium | High | Sprint reviews and UAT ownership |
| Data/BI Lead | Analytics owner | Data definitions, quality, model governance | Medium | High | Data design and KPI working sessions |
| IT Security | Control owner | Access control, audit, privacy compliance | High | Medium | Architecture and security reviews |
| Parent Representative | Advisory stakeholder | Clear process and responsiveness | Low | Medium | Advisory interviews |
| Teacher Representative | Intake contributor | Referral simplicity and feedback loop | Medium | Medium | Workshops and pilot feedback |

## Power interest matrix

| Quadrant | Stakeholders | Management strategy |
|---|---|---|
| High power / High interest | Superintendent, Assistant Superintendent, Operations Director, Counseling Director | Manage closely |
| High power / Low interest | IT Security, Legal/Privacy | Keep satisfied |
| Low power / High interest | Counselors, Support Coordinators, Principals | Keep informed and involve in design |
| Low power / Low interest | Parent and student advisory groups | Monitor and collect periodic feedback |
