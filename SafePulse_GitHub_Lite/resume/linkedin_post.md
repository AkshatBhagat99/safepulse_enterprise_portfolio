# LinkedIn Post

I recently built a portfolio case study called SafePulse, a School Well-being Operations & Analytics Platform focused on improving student support operations.

The goal was not to build a diagnosis or prediction tool. Instead, I designed it like an enterprise operations platform that helps schools manage support requests, counselor workload, response SLAs, follow-ups, escalations, and executive reporting.

The project includes a BRD, FRD, stakeholder analysis, process flows, data architecture, 250k+ synthetic records, 100+ SQL queries, Power BI dashboard specs, KPI logic, UAT cases, and resume-ready project storytelling.

This helped me bring together Business Analysis, Data Analytics, SQL, dashboard design, requirements traceability, and product thinking into one realistic enterprise case study.
