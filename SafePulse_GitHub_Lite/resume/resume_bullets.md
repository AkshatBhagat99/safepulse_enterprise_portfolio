## STAR story

**Situation:** Schools lacked consistent visibility into student support case workload, SLA breaches, counselor capacity, and executive trends.

**Task:** Create an enterprise-grade operating model and analytics framework that could standardize workflows and support leadership decisions without clinical prediction.

**Action:** Developed discovery artifacts, stakeholder analysis, BRD/FRD, user stories, process flows, data architecture, 250k+ synthetic dataset, SQL KPI library, dashboard specifications, and testing artifacts.

**Result:** Produced a consulting-style portfolio project demonstrating end-to-end BA and DA delivery from business problem framing through data model, reporting, testing, and executive storytelling.
